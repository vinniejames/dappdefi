## Overview

Loopscale is a modular lending platform on Solana built for next-generation on-chain assets — LSTs, RWAs, structured tokens. Markets are configurable and isolated, and the protocol routes orders to deliver competitive rates.

The positioning is similar in spirit to Morpho Blue's permissionless-market design adapted to Solana's runtime, with an emphasis on listing assets that less flexible markets can't accept.
