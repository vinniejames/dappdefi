## Overview

Makina is a DeFi execution-engine framework that lets sophisticated operators run on-chain yield strategies with explicit policy controls. The platform's contracts enforce strategy boundaries — allowed venues, asset universes, leverage limits — while letting operators pursue aggressive yield within them.

The positioning is similar to Mellow's vault framework but emphasizes execution flexibility for operator-led strategies.
