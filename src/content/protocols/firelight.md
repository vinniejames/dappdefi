## Overview

Firelight is an XRP liquid staking platform on Flare. Users lock XRP on the XRPL, and Firelight mints a corresponding LST on Flare that accrues XRP staking rewards while remaining transferable and usable in Flare DeFi.

The project leverages Flare's FAssets bridge stack to bring XRP into a smart-contract environment with a yield-bearing wrapper, similar to how Lido did for ETH.
