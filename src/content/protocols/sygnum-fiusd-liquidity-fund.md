## Overview

FIUSD is a tokenization of Fidelity International's USD Money Market Fund issued by Sygnum Bank, a regulated Swiss digital-asset bank. The fund holds short-duration U.S. Treasuries and money-market instruments; on-chain holders gain regulated exposure through the tokenized share class.

The product is part of the regulated-tokenized-money-market tier alongside BUIDL and BENJI.
