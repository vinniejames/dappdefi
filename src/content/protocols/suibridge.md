Sui Bridge is the native bridge between Sui and Ethereum, designed to move ETH, ERC-20s, and Sui-native assets between the two chains. Security is anchored by the Sui validator set, with the bridge contracts as the canonical entry point into Sui DeFi.

The bridge is part of Sui's broader effort to deepen interoperability with Ethereum's asset universe.
